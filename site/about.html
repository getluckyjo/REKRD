The name

REKRD, as in record. Not the one on a leaderboard — the personal one. Feeling better today than yesterday. Playing the back nine as well as the front. Getting off a red-eye and still being a person.

The .io in our name says the rest: what you put in is what you get out.

The formula

It started with a chef who doesn’t sit in kitchens — an Ironman athlete who formulates products instead, and got tired of hydration powders full of fillers, dyes and enough sodium to pickle you.

So we made our own: electrolytes in honest doses, coconut water powder, ancient Himalayan salt with trace minerals, L-glutamine for a formula that’s gentle enough for every day. Then we paid to have every batch third-party tested, because “trust us” isn’t an ingredient.

The tube

Remember tennis-ball tubes? The good kind — the ones that popped when you opened them and lived on a shelf for twenty years afterwards. That’s the standard we set for our packaging.

Your first REKRD arrives in a tube designed to be kept: on the kitchen counter, on the desk, in the golf bag. When it’s empty, refill it. We think the nicest thing a brand can be is useful furniture.

Where we play

We’re not chasing marathon runners — they’re well looked after. REKRD is for the halfway house, the fourth set, the festival, the flight, and the standing 6am meeting with your own reflection.
