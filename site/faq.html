The product

What exactly is REKRD?

A clean hydration powder in single-serve sachets. Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium), coconut water powder, ancient Himalayan salt with trace minerals, L-glutamine, vitamin C and zinc. No fillers, no artificial colours, flavours or sweeteners. Mix one sachet into 500–600 ml of cold water, once a day.

Do I need to do sport to use it?

No — that’s rather the point. Most people run mildly dehydrated on nothing more strenuous than air-conditioning, coffee and life. REKRD works for golf and padel days, but also for long flights, hot days, fasting mornings and late nights.

Why only one a day?

Our formula is balanced for daily use — enough electrolytes to work, not so much sodium that you feel like biltong. One sachet a day is the ritual. On very hot or very active days, listen to your body (and your water bottle).

What does “third-party tested” mean?

We pay an independent laboratory to test our batches — for purity and for the label matching what’s actually in the sachet. It costs us real money and we think it’s the cost of being taken seriously.

Is there sugar or caffeine in it?

No added sugar (0,0 g per sachet) and no caffeine. It won’t spike you and it won’t crash you.

What’s the shelf life?

12 months from manufacture. Each pack is marked with its best-before date. Store below 25 °C in a cool, dry place — the tube is rather good at that.

Delivery

How long does delivery take?

1–3 working days in major metros, 2–5 working days regionally, via the Bob Go courier network. You’ll get tracking the moment your order ships.

What does delivery cost?

Free on orders over R500 — which the 30-sachet tube clears on its own. Under R500, delivery is charged at checkout (from R60, depending on where you are).

Do you deliver outside South Africa?

Not yet. For the record: it’s on the list.

Refills & subscriptions

How does the refill subscription work?

Your first order arrives in the collectible tube. After that, refill packs arrive on a schedule you choose — every 4, 5 or 6 weeks — at subscriber pricing. Pause, skip or cancel anytime, no interrogation. Launching soon; join the newsletter to be first.

Can I buy a single flavour?

Not yet — the first run is the mixed tube (six of each flavour) and the 5-sachet starter. Single-flavour packs are planned once we know which flavour you all fight over.

Orders & returns
