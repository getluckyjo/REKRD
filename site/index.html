Questions, answered

Fair questions.

What exactly is REKRD?

A clean hydration powder in single-serve sachets. Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium), coconut water powder, ancient Himalayan salt with trace minerals, L-glutamine, vitamin C and zinc. No fillers, no artificial colours, flavours or sweeteners. Mix one sachet into 500 ml of cold water, once a day.

Do I need to do sport to use it?

No. Most people run mildly dehydrated on nothing more strenuous than air-conditioning, coffee and life. REKRD works for golf and padel days, but also for long flights, hot days, fasting mornings and late nights.

Why only one a day?

Our formula is balanced for daily use — enough electrolytes to work, not so much sodium that you feel like biltong. On very hot or very active days, listen to your body (and your water bottle).

What does “third-party tested” mean?

We pay an independent laboratory to test our batches — for purity and for the label matching what’s actually in the sachet. It costs us real money and we think it’s the cost of being taken seriously.

Is there sugar or caffeine in it?

No added sugar (0,0 g per sachet) and no caffeine. It won’t spike you and it won’t crash you.

How long does delivery take?

1–3 working days in major metros, 2–5 working days regionally, via the Bob Go courier network. Free on orders over R500 — which the tube clears on its own. You’ll get tracking the moment your order ships.

How do refill subscriptions work?

Your first order arrives in the collectible tube. After that, refill packs arrive on a schedule you choose, at subscriber pricing. Pause, skip or cancel anytime, no interrogation. Launching soon — join the newsletter to be first.

Something arrived damaged. Now what?

Email hello@rekrd.io with your order number and a photo, and we’ll sort it out — replacement or refund, your call. Full details in our shipping & returns policy.
