Shipping & returns

Delivery

We ship across South Africa via the Bob Go courier network. Orders placed before 12:00 on a working day are usually dispatched the same day.

Delivery times & costs
Orders over R500Free
Orders under R500Calculated at checkout (from R60)
Major metros (JHB, PTA, CPT, DBN)1–3 working days
Regional & outlying areas2–5 working days

You’ll receive a tracking link by email as soon as your order ships. If your order hasn’t arrived within the windows above, email hello@rekrd.io and we’ll chase it down.

Returns

Because REKRD is a consumable food product, we can’t accept returns of opened packs — but we stand behind every order:

  • Damaged or incorrect orders: email us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and a photo. We’ll replace the order or refund you in full — your choice.
  • Unopened products: may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a refund of the purchase price. Return shipping is for your account unless the error was ours.
  • Quality concerns: if something tastes or looks off, stop using it and contact us immediately with the batch number printed on the pack.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method via our payment processor (Paystack) within 5–10 working days of approval.

Questions

Anything unclear? hello@rekrd.io — an actual human will reply within one working day.
