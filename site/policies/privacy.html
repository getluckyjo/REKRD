Privacy policy

The short version: we collect only what we need to get REKRD to your door, we don’t sell your information to anyone, and you can ask us what we hold about you at any time.

Who we are

REKRD (Pty) Ltd, Pretoria, South Africa (“REKRD”, “we”, “us”). We are the responsible party for personal information processed through this website, as defined by the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). Contact: hello@rekrd.io.

What we collect

  • Order information: name, email, phone number, delivery address, order history.
  • Payment information: processed entirely by Shopify and Paystack. We never see or store your card details.
  • Newsletter signups: your email address, only if you opt in.
  • Technical basics: standard website analytics (pages visited, device type) to keep the site working well.

How we use it

To process and deliver your orders, to respond when you contact us, to send the newsletter you asked for (unsubscribe with one click, any time), and to meet our legal and tax obligations. That’s it.

Who we share it with

Only the service providers who make the shop work: Shopify (e-commerce platform), Paystack (payments), Bob Go and its courier partners (delivery), and our email platform (newsletter). Each is bound to protect your information. We do not sell or rent personal information to anyone.

Your rights

Under POPIA you may ask us to confirm what personal information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it (subject to legal record-keeping requirements). Email hello@rekrd.io. You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to run the cart and checkout, and basic analytics cookies to understand how the site is used. No creepy cross-site tracking.

Changes

If we change this policy we’ll update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be flagged on the site.
